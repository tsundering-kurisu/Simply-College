Clubs of IIITA
Sarasva, The 'Lit'erary Society: If you're into writing, poetry, debating, or quizzing, Sarasva is your go-to club! From organizing North India’s biggest MUNs to hosting main-stage events during Effe, it’s all about creativity and expression. They also publish campus magazines like IMHO (English) and Swacchanda (Hindi). With plenty of volunteer opportunities, it’s easy to join—just be confident in the interview. Love talking and late-night discussions? You’ll fit right in!
AMS members are the backbone of every event, managing sound, visuals, lighting, and the auditorium console with finesse. They’re also the ones behind those enviable DSLR-level DPs everyone loves. If you’re into photography, photo editing, or video editing, this is the place for you. Plus, AMS members earn massive respect for their dedication—and the amazing DPs they churn out for everyone! For more info click Here
If music runs in your soul, Virtuosi is the place to shine. Whether you play an instrument or have a voice that captivates, this society celebrates Rock (Indian and International) and niche music styles. Even if you’ve never performed before, give it a shot—the supportive seniors are always on the lookout for potential. There’s no harm in trying! Even if your performance isn’t perfect, the seniors ensure you leave with encouragement and a smile. for more info click Here.
Genetix Dance Society: Where Passion Meets Rhythm If dancing fuels your spirit, the Genetix Dance Society is your go-to space. From learning new moves to perfecting your style, this vibrant group is always buzzing. Bonus: Their air-conditioned dance room ensures you can groove in comfort. While auditions are competitive, don’t hesitate to showcase your unique style—your baarati moves might just win over the seniors!
GeekHaven is the backbone of IIIT Allahabad’s tech culture, organizing some of the coolest tech events and workshops. It comprises several wings like FOSS, Design, Cyber Security, Machine Learning, and Competitive Programming. From mad coding sprees to mastering tech skills, GeekHaven members live and breathe technology. It’s the perfect place to collaborate, learn, and showcase your passion for innovation while being part of a thriving tech community! find more info Here.
Nirmiti handles every artistic decoration on campus, from breathtaking fest themes to stunning event visuals. They’re the creative force ensuring every occasion looks extraordinary. The work requires a blend of creativity and hands-on effort. Pro tip: Be ready to showcase your drawings, sketches, or artwork during the interview—they’ll want to see your artistic talent!
Rangtarangini, the dramatics society, is home to some of the most talented actors, directors, and screenwriters you’ll find. Known for their hard work and passion, their performances always fill the halls with energy and applause. Pro tip: Everyone should try the dramatics auditions—it’s an unforgettable experience! Prior acting experience isn’t necessary; just be confident, have fun, and face the audience. You might just make it in! Email: rangtarangini.society@iiita.ac.in
