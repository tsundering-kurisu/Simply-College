IIIT Allahabad's campus and facilities.
  It all begins here :D
"As a new saga begins for every dreamer, the gates of IIIT Allahabad stand tall, not just as an entrance, but as a passage for legends, welcoming all to claim their destiny."
You may enter but, take a step forward with purpose. But before you journey ahead, turn back and look up at the top of the gate. There lies a message, a silent reminder of the responsibility that awaits beyond these walls.
Welcome to the gates of IIITA.
The auditorium and library of IIIT Allahabad are two key hubs of campus life, both fully air-conditioned for comfort. The auditorium, with a seating capacity of 1200+, hosts events like fests, tech summits, and cultural performances. The library, open 24/7, houses 70,000+ extensive e-resources and books, providing a quiet space for learning and research. Together, they form the heart of academic and extracurricular activities on campus.
IIIT Allahabad’s food facilities include a Dewsis Cafeteria, the heart of campus dining, offering a variety of affordable meals in a spacious setting. Along with it, the Amul Parlour serves refreshing dairy treats, and the Nescafe Shop provides a perfect spot for coffee and snacks. Together, they keep students fueled and refreshed throughout the day.
  • Free Bus Ride - The institute provides a convenient free bus service to the city, running twice a day. Students can hop on the bus at the dedicated campus stop, offering them a hassle-free and cost-free way to travel to and from the city for errands or leisure.
  • Temple - A serene Hindu temple on campus offers a peaceful space for cultural and spiritual activities, providing students with an opportunity for reflection and connection to their traditions.
  • Canara ATM - A Canara ATM on campus ensures easy access to cash for students, saving them time and effort when handling financial transactions.
  • Small Shopping Complex - The shopping complex provides students with essential items, snacks, and small goods, offering convenience right on campus for everyday needs.
  • Students Activity Center -A dedicated space for students to engage in their hobbies and unwind. This area fosters creativity and relaxation, allowing students to enjoy personal pursuits.
IIIT Allahabad offers a range of sports facilities, including a swimming pool with heating in winter and grounds for most of sports. The institute also hosts vibrant sports festivals, providing students with opportunities to stay active and showcase their skills.
  • Health Care Center- The IIIT Allahabad Health Centre (IHC), operational 24/7 since 2005, provides comprehensive medical care to the institute's members. It offers services from allopathic doctors, gynecologists, orthopedicians, homeopathic doctors, and other medical staff. The center is equipped with essential facilities like a mini OT, ECG machine, oxygen cylinders, and ambulances for emergency care. It maintains a stock of 194 medicines and offers 58 different pathological tests. Additionally, IHC provides a medical claim that covers treatment at hospitals across Allahabad for all students.
  • Visitor Hostel- IIIT Allahabad provides accommodation for visiting guests with 3 visitor hostel blocks, offering 40 air-conditioned rooms and 8 suites. All rooms are equipped with computers and come with 24-hour backup power and water supply for a comfortable stay.
  • PMC (Piya Milan Chauraha) Located between the boys' and girls' hostels, PMC is where students meet in a ceremonious fashion. Marked by a large streetlight, it’s a popular spot for quiet moments, despite the insects buzzing around in summer. It’s where many students share romantic moments, often with a touch of humor about the occasional ghost sightings.
  • The Clocktower at IIIT Allahabad is known for its loud chimes every hour, followed by "DONGS!" and sometimes a "Jai Hind!" It stands beneath the institute’s logo, lit by changing colored lights. Though its noise can be a disturbance, it’s also a romantic spot for midnight walks, becoming an accepted part of campus life.
