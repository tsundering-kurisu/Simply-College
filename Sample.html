Famous alumini you might know
Harsha 'Humblefool' Suryanarayana
B.Tech.IT, (2006)
Harsha Suryanarayana, known as Harsha or shashank21, was India’s first red coder and a legendary competitive programmer. His brilliance shone on platforms like Topcoder and Codeforces. His untimely passing in 2014 remains a profound loss to the programming world.
