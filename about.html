Simply College is a comprehensive college database website designed to showcase the top colleges and assist students during the challenging process of selecting the right one.
Created by Yash Kapadiya with contributions from everyone listed in the credits section, this platform aims to make your college decision more meaningful and informed.
You can also become a part of this project by visiting the Contribute section.
This website offers detailed insights into campuses, facilities, clubs, festivals, and placements. Additionally, it provides information about the cities where the colleges are located, along with details about hostels and other essentials.
We hope Simply College proves helpful to all students, especially those navigating the post-exam college selection phase.
