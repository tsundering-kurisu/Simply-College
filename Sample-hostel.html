Hostels of IIITA
  Boys Hostel
The Boys Hostel at IIIT-Allahabad consists of five buildings (BH1–BH5) with a capacity for 1850 students, located across from the main campus. It offers air-conditioned mess facilities, sports like volleyball and badminton, and a gym.

For 1st and 2nd-year students, rooms are shared with one roommate. In special cases with available rooms, 1st and 2nd-year students may get single rooms at higher rent. From the 3rd year onwards, students are assigned single rooms. To ensure safety, the 1st-year boys' hostel is kept separate from the seniors. Ragging is strictly prohibited.
  Girls Hostel
"Living in the IIIT-Allahabad hostel helps you build friendships and balance studies with relaxation."
The Girls Hostel consists of GH1, GH2, and GH3, housing around 500 students. It features air-conditioned mess and gym facilities, with a basketball and badminton court outside GH1. Students enjoy a homely environment with 24/7 caretaker guidance. The wardens ensure proper functioning and safety, with regulated timings for entry and exit.
Hostel Food
The mess at IIIT-Allahabad is air-conditioned and provides a comfortable dining experience. A student-run mess committee plays a key role in deciding the menu, with changes made occasionally based on majority preferences. Unfavored items are often replaced to ensure students enjoy their meals. For late-night cravings, students can visit the night canteen for additional food options.
